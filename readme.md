# 🔬 HSTU Research Society

**HSTU Research Society** is a student-led academic and research community dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and scientific thinking among students of Hajee Mohammad Danesh Science and Technology University (HSTU).

Our mission is to create an environment where students can explore research, develop technical skills, collaborate across disciplines, and contribute meaningful solutions to real-world problems.

---

## 🌟 About Us

The HSTU Research Society aims to bridge the gap between **academic learning** and **practical research experience**. We encourage students from all departments to participate in research activities, open-source development, and knowledge sharing.

We focus on:

* 📚 Promoting undergraduate research culture
* 🧠 Developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills
* 🤝 Encouraging interdisciplinary collaboration
* 💻 Supporting open-source and technical innovation
* 📝 Guiding students in research writing and publication

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Provide mentorship for beginners in research
* Organize workshops, seminars, and research discussions
* Build collaborative research projects
* Help students learn academic writing and publishing
* Create open-source tools and datasets
* Connect students with researchers and professionals
